What is Pantastic?

Have you recently bought a new iPhone or upgraded to the latest iOS firmware? If so, you now have the ability to take beautiful panoramas stitched together automatically and in real-time. But panoramas are so large in nature they are difficult to view on your tiny iPhone screen. Bring your panoramas to life!

With Pantastic, you can quickly and easily enjoy your panorams in all their glory with a beautiful, 8" x 36" print. Become the artist you know you are. Express your imagination. Be Pantastic.

Meet Pantastic

FAQ

Q: How much does a Pantastic Pano cost?

A: It costs $15.

Q: What comes with my purchase?

A: One 8" x 36" panorama.

Q: What if I want to order a different size panorama?

A: At the moment, we only have the capacity to print at one size. If you would like to order a different size, contact us at hi@bepantastic.com.

Q: What if I want to order more than one panorama?

A: Currently, the only way to do so is to place multiple separate orders. In the future, we hope to make this process easier.

Q: How do I take a panorama on my iPhone?

A: It's really easy. Simply launch the Camera App, press "Options" at the top of the sceen, and select "Panorama." Then follow the on-screen instructions to take your panorama. Here is a video if you're having trouble.

Q: Do I have to upload a panorama taken from my iPhone to be printed?

A: Yes, at the moment, we only support the printing of panoramas taken with iPhones. If you would like a different size panorama to be printed, contact us at hi@bepantastic.com.
