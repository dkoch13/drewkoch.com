Meet Pantastic

About the Founder

Drew Koch is the Founder and CEO of Pantastic. Drew grew up in Houston, Texas, and his interest in photography and printing started to develop in high school at The Kinkaid School. Without the support of his amazing photography teacher at Kinkaid for four years, Mr. David Veselka, none of this would be possible. Now a senior at Washington University in St. Louis, Drew looks to bring everyone's panoramas to life with the launch of his first company, Pantastic. He is very excited about the opportunities and challenges ahead.

Made in St. Louis

Pantastic prints, boxes, and ships all panoramas from their facilities in the heart of St. Louis. While it used to be the Gateway to the West, St. Louis is now shifting its focus to technology. With many resources for entrepreneurs in place, St. Louis is starting to shape its position as a Midwest stronghold for technology. To learn more, check out innovatestl.org.

Printers

At Pantastic, every single print goes through our state-of-the-art Epson photo printer. We know quality, and we know the importance of a good printer. That's why we didn't skimp on a cheap one. In fact, we went with the best. Oh, yea, and we use the highest quality archival inkjet roll paper to boot. Don't worry about your photo fading during your lifetime.

FAQ

Q: How much does a Pantastic Panorama cost?

A: It costs only $25.

Q: What comes with my purchase?

A: One 8" x 36" panorama.

Q: What if I want to order a different size panorama?

A: At the moment, we only have the capacity to print at one size. If you would like to order a different size, contact us at hi@pantastic.co.

Q: What if I want to order more than one panorama?

A: Currently, the only way to do so is to place multiple separate orders. In the future, we hope to make this process easier.

Q: How do I take a panorama on my iPhone?

A: It's really easy. Simply launch the Camera App, press "Options" at the top of the sceen, and select "Panorama." Then follow the on-screen instructions to take your panorama. Here is a video if you're having trouble.

Q: Do I have to upload a panorama taken from my iPhone to be printed?

A: Yes, at the moment, we only support the printing of panoramas taken with iPhones. If you would like a different size panorama to be printed, contact us at hi@pantastic.co.
